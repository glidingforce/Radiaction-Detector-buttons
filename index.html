Step 2Find Common + DMG pins
1Keep the buttons array connected to the PCB. Disconnect the PCB from the C-arm (unplug the C-arm connector).

2Set multimeter to continuity. Find the two pins that are always in short circuit without pressing any button. These are COMMON and DMG Common.

3Disconnect the buttons array plug. Of the two short-circuit pins:
• The pin that is part of the buttons array wires = COMMON
• The pin that is a separate wire (DMG harness) = DMG Common

4The other 2 separate wires = DMG Near (panel closer to detector) and DMG Far.
Test DMG Common against those 2 wires -- the one that beeps = DMG Near.

5Tap any pin below to fill the fields, or type directly.
